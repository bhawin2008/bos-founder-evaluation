Founder Revenue Blueprint

About this event

Most IT founders know how to build great products — but struggle to build a predictable revenue engine. This workshop changes that. In two focused hours, you'll map out a complete pipeline system tailored to your specific business model, market, and growth stage.

This isn't theory. You'll leave with a documented revenue blueprint that covers your entire sales journey — from initial awareness to closed deal — with specific actions, metrics, and timelines for each stage.

Event intro video coming soon

What you'll learn

  • How to identify and document your ideal revenue channels (inbound vs. outbound vs. partner)
  • The BOS Revenue Canvas — a visual framework for mapping your entire pipeline
  • Setting up lead scoring and qualification criteria that actually work
  • Creating conversion triggers at each stage of your funnel
  • Building a weekly revenue rhythm that keeps your pipeline active without consuming your calendar
  • Real metrics that matter: what to track, what to ignore, and how to set realistic targets

Who this is for

This workshop is designed for IT founders who have a product or service with some traction (even minimal revenue) but lack a systematic approach to growing that revenue predictably. If you're tired of feast-or-famine cycles and want a repeatable system, this session is for you.

Format

This is a hands-on, interactive workshop — not a passive webinar. You'll work on your own revenue blueprint during the session, with live feedback from the facilitator and fellow founders. Come prepared with your current revenue numbers and growth targets.
