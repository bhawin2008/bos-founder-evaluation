What you'll build

BOS Basics isn't a course you watch — it's a system you build. Over 8 weeks, you'll create the foundational operating systems that every serious IT company needs. Each week focuses on one system, with a live session, framework template, and specific implementation actions.

By the end of the program, you'll have a documented operating system covering your revenue pipeline, market positioning, decision-making processes, delivery workflow, and weekly execution rhythm. These aren't theoretical — they're the same systems used by BOS founders generating $100K–$500K/month.

8-week curriculum

Week 1 — Founder Clarity. Define your ideal business model, revenue targets, and 12-month vision using the BOS Clarity Canvas.

Week 2 — Market Positioning. Use the Positioning Playbook to identify your niche, differentiation, and messaging that resonates with your ideal client.

Week 3 — Revenue Engine. Build your pipeline system from scratch using the Revenue Canvas. Map awareness, nurture, and close stages with specific actions.

Week 4 — Sell Without Selling. Design your inbound system: content strategy, lead magnets, and conversion triggers that bring clients to you.

Week 5 — Decision Framework. Install the Founder Decision OS for making high-stakes calls with confidence. Eliminate analysis paralysis permanently.

Week 6 — Delivery Systems. Create operational workflows for client delivery, project management, and quality assurance that don't depend on you.

Week 7 — Execution Rhythm. Build your Weekly CEO Operating Rhythm: time blocks, metrics reviews, team check-ins, and strategic thinking slots.

Week 8 — Integration & Launch. Connect all systems into a unified operating system. Set 90-day execution targets and graduation into the BOS community.

Who this is for

BOS Basics is designed for IT founders who have a product or service with some traction but lack structured business systems. You might be a technical founder who's great at building but struggling with the business side. Or a service-based founder who's grown through referrals but needs a real growth engine.

Ideal candidates are pre-revenue to $50K/month founders, solo founders or small teams (1–5 people), and anyone who's tired of running their business on improvisation.
