Signal Framework

How culture signals are measured and interpreted

Growth Signals

◉ x1
Missed Deadline
If a task passes its due date, 1 growth signal is automatically recorded. This surfaces where support or capacity adjustments may be needed.
◉ x1
Needs Support
If a task is completed but the result needs improvement, 1 growth signal is recorded. This is a coaching cue, not a judgment.
◉ x3
Critical Incident
For serious process failures, 3 growth signals are recorded. This indicates a systemic issue requiring attention, not individual blame.

Strength Signals

◉ x1
Completed with Excellence
When a task is completed and meets all expectations, 1 strength signal is recorded. Recognizes consistent, reliable delivery.
◉ x2
Exceptional Contribution
When work exceeds expectations and creates outsized impact, 2 strength signals are recorded. Celebrates innovation and initiative.

Culture Mechanics

♛ +1ea
Leadership Signal
Mentoring, cross-team collaboration, or being positively referenced by peers earns +1 strength signal per criterion. Measures leadership maturity, not just output.
◉ note
Monthly Attention Signal
2+ growth signals in a single month triggers an attention badge. This is a pattern indicator, not a punishment — it invites a conversation.
↻ -50%
Signal Recovery
2 consecutive clean months (no growth signals) reduces all older growth signals to 50% weight. Sustained improvement is rewarded with a clean slate path.

How It Works

1
Assign tasks to team members with clear expectations and due dates.
2
Automatic detection surfaces tasks past due. A growth signal is recorded as a coaching cue.
3
Log critical incidents when process failures occur. This captures systemic patterns, not blame.
4
Review completions thoughtfully. Rate quality to assign strength or growth signals.
5
Recognize leadership — use the crown button to acknowledge mentoring, collaboration, and cultural contributions.
6
Signal Recovery — 2 clean months automatically halves old growth signal weight. Monthly Attention activates when patterns emerge.
7
Read the dashboard with a growth mindset. Trends show Accelerating/Steady/Needs Attention. Support Signals invite conversations, not consequences.
